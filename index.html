The ladder

Each level adds one technique on top of the last. Every card links to the technique, the exact prompts that drove it, and the live result from both models.

1

Grab and go

Treat Claude like a chatbot: no references, no skills, no assets. The baseline everything else improves on.
2

Screenshots & references

Show, don't tell. One reference image transfers visual intent almost losslessly — paragraphs of adjectives can't.
3

Design skills

Install packaged design expertise so the model knows what good looks like — then it stops eating crayons and starts playing the piano.
4

UI snipping

Don't re-engineer the fireplace — copy it. Production-grade components from 21st.dev, Magic UI, or CodePen drop straight in.
5

AI image & video

Generate the media you don't have. A one-prompt product film is what separates 'AI website' from 'production website'.
6

Data & brand research

A beautiful site that doesn't convert is a Ferrari with no engine. Ground the design and copy in real data — scraped live with Firecrawl.
7

Design extraction

The strongest lever: extract the full design language of a site you love, and rebuild your product in that language. Translation, not cloning.
